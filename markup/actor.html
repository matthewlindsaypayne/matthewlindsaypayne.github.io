Villain. Fool. Schemer. Dreamer.

Trained at the Moscow Art Theatre School and Everyman Theatre, I am currently portraying V. in The Mesmeric Revelations of Edgar Allan Poe, an immersive show in Baltimore on the life, times, and work of Poe. I recently played Vanya in Uncle Vanya while studying with the MXAT and Franz in The Trap at Ambassador Theatre Company. I am a stage combat enthusiast, and a student of Ritterschule Baltimore, an historical martial combat school. I am currently based in Baltimore, but am looking to move in the near future.
